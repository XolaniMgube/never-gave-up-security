About Us

Never Give Up Security is a proudly black-owned South African security company, established in 2021 by Sibusiso Thwayine. The company was founded with a clear purpose: to provide reliable, professional security services that meet the everyday safety needs of homes, businesses, and events.

Registered under Company Registration Number: 2021/121578/07, Never Give Up Security operates 24 hours a day, with its physical offices based in Germiston, Gauteng, while delivering services across various areas as required.

We offer a range of guarding solutions, including residential, commercial, indoor, outdoor, and function guarding. Our team is trained to maintain a visible, disciplined presence while upholding high standards of professionalism and accountability at all times.

At Never Give Up Security, protection is more than a service — it’s a commitment. We take pride in doing the job properly and never compromising when it comes to safety.
