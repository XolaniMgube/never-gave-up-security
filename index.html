ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Reliable Protection
You Can Trust

Never Gave Up Security is a proudly black-owned South African security company, established in 2021 by Sizwe Madonsela. The company was founded with a clear purpose: to provide reliable, professional security services that meet the everyday safety needs of homes, businesses, and events.

The company operates 24 hours a day, with its physical offices based in Germiston, Gauteng, while delivering services across multiple areas as required.

Our services include residential, commercial, indoor, outdoor, and function guarding. Our guards maintain a visible, disciplined presence while upholding high standards of professionalism and accountability.
